Stretch, Strength and SMART Goal

Stretch

My stretch is writing essays. I find essays challenging to write and hard to make my thoughts flow. I feel that my stretch is still a stretch, I still don't feel confident after writing my essays and it isn't easier to write them as well. I am still getting consistent 85-88 grades on my essays. I will improve on this by writing a more descriptive thinking map so I can get all my ideas down and organize them so my essay will flow and have detailed analysis.

Strength

My strength is not procrastinating. My new Strength of not procrastinating has helped me on many projects such as annotations. By spacing out my annotations and doing some every day, I get more free time and am able to do activities I love. My strength will also help me in my future career because I can space out my assignments and won't have to do them at the last minute.

SMART Goal

My SMART goal was to improve and feel more confident after writing my essays. I have tried to work to my goal by writing a more descriptive thinking map. By having a detailed thinking map I can organize my thoughts so my essay will flow and have analysis as well. I will achieve my goal by spending more time on my essays and I will add more analysis. I will also annotate documents and books so I can get my ideas down and use that for my essay.
